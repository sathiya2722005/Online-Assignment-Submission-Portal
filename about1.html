ABOUT OUR PORTAL

Objectives

This system aims to reduce manual paperwork, save time for students and faculty, and ensure transparency in assignment submission and evaluation processes.

Student Module

Students can securely register and login, view assignment details, upload assignment files, track submission status, and view marks and feedback provided by faculty.

Faculty Module

Faculty members can review submitted assignments, download files, evaluate student work, assign marks, and provide feedback efficiently through the system.

Admin Module

The administrator manages the entire system including student and faculty accounts, assignment creation, monitoring submissions, and generating reports.

Advantages

  • Paperless and eco-friendly platform
  • Secure and centralized assignment storage
  • Easy access anytime, anywhere
  • Reduces manual workload
  • Improves academic efficiency

Conclusion

This project provides a smart digital solution for modern educational institutions, making assignment management more organized, transparent, and effective.
